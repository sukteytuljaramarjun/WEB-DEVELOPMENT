THE BEGINNIG OF ANOTHER ERA

the gksk foundation, which is involved in serving the society in education through its disha centers, decided to do something to ensure that every child is
receiving an education without encountering any difficulties in light of the consequences brought about by the coroni in 2020 in the educational field.

the findings of the organization's survey on the status of children attending school disturbed the entire team because many of the students left their
schools because their families couldn't pay the high school fees during the pandemic.

kandru vinod kumar, the founder and chairman, then decided to rewrite the education system of india and decided to start education institute which will charge
just a small amount of rupees of 5000 per child for any class from nursery to tenth and decided to not compromise in the quality.

after putting in a lot of effort over the course of several months, the team was successful in opening their first school, "bala sri chaitanya model school,"
in karwan hyderabad. the team got off to a memorable start with a strength of more than 600 in just their first year.

along with the academics, the organisation provided corporate level infrastructure and facilities like digital classes, libraries, science labs, and computer labs.
additionally, the organisation also taught the students lessons in personality development for their overall growth.

like usual, the team's goal this time is to serve a number of people in the community, and as part of that effort, they are working to expand their school branches
in the upcoming academic years.
