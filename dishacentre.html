GKSK FOUNDATION
(GURU KRUPA SHIKSHANA KENDRAM )

DISHA CENTRES

2017 saw the launch of free evening tutions by the gksk foundation in the slums of hyderabad. in the tuition centres, slum children
received training in academics as well as attention to their overall character development.

by 2022, the ngo has expanded the disha centers to other locations around the nation, including mahbubnagar in telangana and pune in maharashtra.

the disha centres are now composed of 50+ tution centres housing more than 5000 children, carefully managing their education,

mental, and physical fitness, as well as encouraging them in different sports and providing financial support for their higher studies.
