GKSK FOUNDATION
(GURU KRUPA SHIKSHANA KENDRAM )

NGO with a base in Hyderabad is called Guru Krupa Shikshana Kendram, or GKSK FOUNDATION.
Since 2014, the ngo has been active. The organization's mission is to make education accessible
to all people and to educate today's youth about traditional Indian arts.

The nonprofit organisation, led by its visionary chairman and founder KANDRU VINOD KUMAR,
has accomplished remarkable milestones unlike any other with a team of 150 members.The
self-funded nonprofit organisation Guru Krupa Shikshana Kendram is the only one to find novel,
creative solutions to society's problems that will astound people.

The only non-profit organisation that introduces modern kids to traditional Indian arts and teaches
them about their glory isthe GKSK Foundation.People were kept mute by ngo policies and ideas to
improve society.No one else will even dare to attempt the effort that the ngo has done.

There isn't a problem that the ngo hasn't solved. The foundation worked in every area where eliminating
it would improve society as a whole.Whether it's slum development or the trendiest women's empowerment,
the non-profit hasleft its impressive work in its own style, and the effects of this work will be felt for years to come.

The organization's mission is to improve the world as a whole, not just one specific issue.With this vision,
the non-profit organisation works in 360* by addressing all social ills.

The organization's 150-person strong, proud team works tirelessly to ensure the happiness of the families it houses.
The number of families, students, and women receiving assistance from the organisation is growing daily.

Forming an organization and engaging in all the activities that the organization is now involved
in was not the intent of the foundation at the outset.It began as a normal martial arts academy at
the small sri saraswathi shishu mandir high school ground in Karwan Hyderabad, Telangana.

The organisation afterwards began working in several public and private schools where it was conducting
free martial arts camps with the goal of teaching the arts like Taekwondo, Yoga, Karrassem, and self-defence
to every child. Through this, 2000+ students have achieved martial arts proficiency, which is a record in the world.

After setting numerous records in the field of sports, the non-profit organisation expanded into the
area of education by offering free evening classes in slum areas of Hyderabad, later expanding to several
villages in Mahbubnagar , Telangana and include the slums of Pune, Maharashtra.

By imparting self-defense instruction to more than 1000 women, the organisation made a significant
contribution to the history of telangana state. Aiming to make the women self relying for their safety.

A private school with an annual fee of rupees 5000 was established by the founder of the organization
to fulfill the dreams of middle class parents to make their child study in private school at a time
where education is treated as business material to make money.
