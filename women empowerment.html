Assessing the problems facing the women in society
with respect to their safety,

the ngo has decided to run self defence camps
in the degree colleges of telangana state to equip the women with
the courage and confidence to fight for safety on their own.

the ngo began its camping in degree colleges through
government degree college for women, begumpet hyderabad - 500016,
where it teached taekwondo, yoga, karrasamu, and self defence techniques
to 500 girls for 90 days.

then onwards, this campaining under the direction of the gksk foundation's
board, a total of 1000 women are receiving instruction
in self-defense techniques.
